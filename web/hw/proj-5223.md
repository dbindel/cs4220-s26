The 5223 extra project is an opportunity for you to do a little extra
something related to the class and get some feedback on it.  Examples
of good projects could include:

- Implementing a numerical algorithm from a paper;
- Extending a homework or project from the class;
- Doing something for your research (provided it connects to the class
  topics)
  
The project is due at the end of the semester, but should not be
scoped as something that you think you will need to spend the whole
semester on!

For this submission, please just let me know what you are thinking of
working on.  A couple sentences is fine, along with perhaps a pointer
to a paper if you are implementing something from a paper.  This is
mostly an opportunity for me to provide some pointers and feedback.
